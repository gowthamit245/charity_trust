Our Qualities

Regional Blood Transfusion Centre

It has since been designated as Regional Blood Transfusion Centre North West Delhi by State Blood Transfusion council.

Voluntary blood donation

The Blood Bank is based on 100% Voluntary blood donation. 100% Component separation and providing blood components without taking replacement blood donations.

Latest Technology

The Blood Bank has world class equipment based on latest technologies for blood grouping, infection testing and components preparation.

NAT Testing

Lions Blood Bank Shalimar Bagh is the first standalone Blood Bank in the country to start NAT Testing in 2011.

Dedicated Staff

We have a team of dedicated and qualified technical staff who solve difficult cross matching problems referred to us from other blood banks.

Platelet Aphresis

We use best quality consumables e.g. testing kits, reagents, blood collection and apheresis bags.
